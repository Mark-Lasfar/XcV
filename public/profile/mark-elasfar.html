Mark Al-Asfar

From Alexandria to Global Impact

Mark Al-Asfar (Ibrahim Al-Asfar), a Full Stack Developer from Alexandria, Egypt, turned his passion for coding into a global achievement by founding MGZon AI, an AI-driven e-commerce platform inspired by giants like Amazon and Shopify. He began his journey as a student at the Faculty of Computers and Information, Alexandria University, earning a Bachelor's in Information Systems with Very Good honors in 2023.

Founding MGZon AI

In 2023, Mark founded MGZon AI, a platform offering personalized shopping experiences and dynamic vendor stores, achieving over 1.9 million downloads. He developed MGpay, an internal payment system integrated with Stripe, PayPal, and Visa. The platform is supported by integrations with warehouses in the USA, Canada, and China, operating under a U.S. license. Despite frustrations and challenges, Mark's goal was to empower sellers with easy-to-use tools for managing their stores.

MGZon AI's Vision

MGZon AI's vision centers on empowering sellers and buyers through an intelligent platform that combines advanced technology with ease of use. Despite the frustrations and monotony faced during development, Mark persevered, driven by his belief that e-commerce should be accessible and straightforward for all. The goal is to simplify operations for sellers with tools like store customization, dedicated apps, and multi-language and multi-currency support.

Advanced Integrations in MGZon

MGZon AI offers comprehensive integrations to streamline e-commerce operations via a flexible dashboard without prior setup. These include:

Warehouse: Inventory management across warehouses in the USA, Canada, and China with real-time syncing.
Dropshipping: Support for dropshipping with global suppliers to reduce costs and increase efficiency.
Marketplace: An open marketplace platform for sellers to list products effortlessly.
Shipping: Integration with shipping providers like FedEx and DHL for flexible shipping options.
Marketing: Built-in advertising tools integrated with Google Ads and Facebook Ads to boost sales.
Accounting: Integration with QuickBooks and Xero for seamless accounting and invoicing.
CRM: Customer relationship management tools with integrations like Salesforce.
Analytics: Real-time reports powered by Google Analytics and custom tools.
Automation: Automate processes like order management and notifications via Zapier.
Communication: Live chat and email notifications via Twilio.
Education: Educational resources and tutorials via MGZon’s learning platform.
Security: Data protection with AES-256 encryption and GDPR compliance.
Advertising: Manage ad campaigns easily through the dashboard.
Tax: Automated tax calculations with integrations like Avalara.
Other: Custom integrations with platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce.

These integrations can be added effortlessly via the dashboard, with a robust API enabling developers to integrate MGZon with their projects using OAuth 2.0 and multiple endpoints inside and outside the platform. Learn more: OAuth Docs.

passport-mgzon Library and User Experience

MGZon offers the passport-mgzon library for easy authentication (npm install passport-mgzon). Developers can test integrations via the demo, which showcases login and account creation using MGZon authentication (Demo image). After authentication, users are directed to 'Shopping Cart Adventure,' an engaging endless adventure game integrated into the platform. Check the quick start guide: Gist.

MGZon App and Seller Support

MGZon provides an official app for the platform (Download mgzon.apk), with custom apps and subdomains for each seller (e.g., hager-zon.vercel.app). New sellers receive 50 welcome points and a custom store domain upon registration (Seller screenshot).
